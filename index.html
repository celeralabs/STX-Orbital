How STX Orbital Works

1

Authenticated Catalog Retrieval

Daily pull from Space-Track.org using your organization's credentials. Full GP catalog (43,318+ objects as of November 2025).

2

High-Fidelity Propagation

SGP4/SDP4 propagator (U.S. DoD standard) via Skyfield — identical to NASA, ESA, and commercial SSA providers.

3

Conjunction Screening

Coarse/fine filtering with 25 km safety margin. Alerts only on miss distance < 10 km or collision probability > 1×10⁻⁴.

4

STX Orbital Autonomy Engine

Proprietary decision layer evaluates threat severity, fuel budget, payload criticality, and regulatory constraints to deliver executable maneuver recommendations when required.

Validation & Accuracy

Propagator Standard

Alert Thresholds

Aligned with industry best practices: miss distance < 10 km or Pc > 1×10⁻⁴. Near-zero false alarms.
