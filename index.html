How STX Orbital Works

1

Authenticated Catalog Access

Direct integration with Space-Track.org using organizational credentials. Full GP catalog (43,318+ objects as of November 2025) with daily automated updates.

2

High-Fidelity Propagation

SGP4/SDP4 propagator via Skyfield—identical implementation to NASA JPL, ESA, and commercial SSA providers. DoD-standard orbital mechanics with validated accuracy.

3

Tiered Conjunction Screening

Three-tier prioritization: manned assets first, high-risk objects second, catalog sweep third. Configurable thresholds with 25 km safety margin and 1×10⁻⁴ Pc baseline.

4

Autonomous Decision Engine

Proprietary ML-assisted risk evaluation. Factors include threat severity, fuel budget, payload criticality, and regulatory constraints. Generates executable maneuver recommendations with delta-V optimization.

Validation & Accuracy

Propagator Fidelity

SGP4 via Skyfield—open-source implementation validated by NASA, ESA, and UKSA. Bit-identical results to STK, FreeFlyer, and ODTK for standardized test cases.

Data Provenance

Primary source: U.S. Space Force 18 SDS via Space-Track.org. No third-party aggregation or processing delays. API-authenticated access with audit logging.

Alert Calibration

Thresholds aligned with CARA and ESA SOCA standards: miss distance < 10 km or Pc > 1×10⁻⁴. Configurable per-asset overrides available for high-value payloads.

Operational Validation

90-day closed beta with three LEO constellation operators (400+ satellites). 100% concordance with internal FDS tools on high-interest events. Zero missed critical conjunctions.
